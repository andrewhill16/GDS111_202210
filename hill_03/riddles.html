Riddles

1. People make me, change me, save me, and raise me, what am I?

2. What two words added together contain the most letters?

3. What four-letter word can be written forward, backward, or upside down, and can still be read from left to right?

4. What has one head, one foot, and 4 legs?

5. What can you hold in your right hand, but not in your left?

6. What can you catch but not throw?

7. What is the end of everything?

8. The person who makes it has no need of it; the person who buys it has no use for it. The person who uses it can neither see nor feel it. What is it?

9. Two in a corner, one in a room, zero in a house, but one in a shelter. What am I?

10. Take one out and scratch my head, I am now black but once was red. What am I?
